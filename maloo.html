Methi Aloo

      ∥       13 Ratings
 

Ingredients

  • 250 GRAM Potatoes (washed and scrubbed clean), small
  • 500 Gram Fenugreek leaves, finely chopped
  • 1 Teaspoon Fenugreek seeds
  • 3-4 Whole red chillies
  • 1/2 Teaspoon Chilli powder
  • 2 Teaspoon Coriander powder
  • 1/2 Cup Mustard oil
  • Salt to taste
